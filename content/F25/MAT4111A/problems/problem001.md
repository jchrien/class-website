# Problem: sum from subset

<p>Given any five numbers from the set \(\{1, 2, 3, \dots, 8\}\), two of the chosen numbers will add up to \(9\).</p>

(From <i>Proofs: A Long-Form Mathematics Textbook</i> by Jay Cummings.)
