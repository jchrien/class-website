# Problem: Avoiding divisors

<p>Prove that given any \(101\) integers from \(\{1, 2, 3, \dots, 200\}\), at least one of these numbers will divide another.</p>

(From <i>Proofs: A Long-Form Mathematics Textbook</i> by Jay Cummings.)
